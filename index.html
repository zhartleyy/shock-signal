Methodology

How Shock Signal Works

Every morning at 7:00 AM, our AI engine scans global news and runs a four-stage analysis pipeline.

01

Event Detection

We scan Yahoo Finance, Reuters, CNBC, Bloomberg, government data feeds, and commodity trackers for events that could disrupt supply or demand — wars, tariffs, weather, technology shifts, policy changes.

02

Causal Chain Reasoning

For each event, our AI traces the second and third-order supply/demand effects. Example: Iran war → Hormuz closure → oil supply drop → airline fuel costs spike → airline stocks crash.

03

Stock Mapping

We map each supply/demand effect to specific publicly traded stocks based on sector exposure, supply chain position, revenue concentration, and competitive dynamics.

04

Conviction Scoring

Each stock gets a 1-10 conviction score based on directness of causal link, revenue exposure, and how much the move is already priced into the market.
