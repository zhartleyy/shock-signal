How It Works

🔍

Scan

Every morning, we scan global news for events that create sudden shifts in supply or demand for goods, services, or commodities.

🔗

Trace

For each event, we trace the causal chain: what gets scarcer or more abundant, and which companies are directly exposed?

📊

Score

Each stock gets a conviction score (1–10) based on directness of the causal link, revenue exposure, and how much is already priced in.

📡

Signal

Signals are grouped into macro themes, and stocks that appear in multiple signals are flagged as double beneficiaries.
